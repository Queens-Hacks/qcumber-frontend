About Qcumber

The simple (unofficial) course catalog for Queen's students

The original Qcumber was made with passion by a former Queen's undergraduate student named Chris, with the help and support of his friends, and the nudging of one Nissi.

The team has now expanded to include Carey, Phil, Graham and Michael.

Qcumber hopes to fill the severe lack of course-finding tools at Queen's University by making it much easier and faster to browse courses and sections.

Qcumber is Open-Source, which means that we are always looking for new contributors to come and help us out. The code lives under the Queens-Hacks organization on Github, and is split into the scraper, data and frontend.

If you are interested in helping out in any way, you can drop us an email at team@qcumber.ca, or open a issue or pull request on Github.
